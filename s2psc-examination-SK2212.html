Uppsats / research paper
Student performance is assessed through three written assignments: memo (1,5 credits), home-take exam (6 credits) and the final paper, which is a research-proposal (7,5 credits). All three written assessments have to be carried out individually.

Seminarieinlämning / memo
Assignment 1: Memo (1,5 credits). The goal of the assignment is to write a short critical essay "What is quality of government (QoG)? and how the concept of QoG is different from the concept of good governance".
Sid 5 — Se sida →
Muntlig presentation
There are six seminars in total. Seminars 1-5 are discussion-based and focus on the readings assigned to each lecture. The last seminar is devoted to student presentations of their final papers.
Sid 4 — Se sida →
Seminariedeltagande
Seminar attendance is mandatory. Students who are unable to attend a seminar are required to submit a compensatory written memo that critically reviews the literature assigned for that session. The memo must be at least of 1,500 words.
