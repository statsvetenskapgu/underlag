First assessment: multiple-choice exam on the parts of the course literature covered in class so far, taken in class at an occasion with compulsory attendance. … It is worth 3 credits.

Flervalsprov
First assessment: multiple-choice exam … The assessment is done on a scale from 1 to 10 where scores 8-10 is equal to VG, 5-7 is equivalent to G, and lower than 5 is U.
Sid 3 — Se sida →
Uppsats / research paper
Fourth assessment is an independent student paper for the course. … The paper should focus on the relevance of the topic, discussion of existing theories and literature, identification of a testable hypothesis … The length should not be less than 5,000 and not exceed 7,000 words. … worth 6 credits.
Sid 3–4 — Se sida →
Policy brief / policyanalys
Third assessment: students are asked to select a research question among those listed for group presentations and write a policy brief by applying the research question to one topic from a given selection. … The policy brief assignment is max 1,000 words. … worth 3 credits.
Sid 3 — Se sida →
Muntlig presentation
Second assessment: groups of students' oral presentation on a research question provided by the teacher. The group presentation is followed by a discussion with the teacher and other students. … The group presentation is worth 3 credits.
Sid 3 — Se sida →
Seminariedeltagande
The seminar is compulsory, and absence is associated with a written make-up assignment. … 2 Seminars on final paper development on research topics and research design.
