Uppsats / research paper
A written research essay of 3,000 words (8 hec)
Towards the end of the course, you will be able to choose to write on a specific research topic. The research essay should focus on a research problem and issue that incorporates at least two of the three themes that this course has focused on, i.e. security, trade, and migration. … Writing the research essay offers you the opportunity to explore in-depth a particular issue and research problem that you find particularly interesting.

Seminarieinlämning / memo
Active participation in three seminars with written assignments (3 x 2 hec)
There are three seminars on this course and participation is mandatory. You are required to actively prepare for and participate in all three seminars, including written assignments. Each seminar is evaluated with the grade High Pass/Pass or Fail.

Muntlig presentation
An oral seminar presentation (1 hec)
You will be required to make one oral seminar presentation on this course. In preparation for the seminar, you will be working with around 4-5 students and prepare a collective group presentation based on the specific instructions for each seminar. The seminars are student-led and based on teamwork. The main responsibility for prompting an engaging seminar discussion lies with the presenters.

Seminariedeltagande
There are three seminars on this course and participation is mandatory. You are required to actively prepare for and participate in all three seminars, including written assignments.
If you are absent from a theme seminar but submitted a paper, you need to complete an extra assignment and write a text of 1,000 words that is a critical analysis of two additional academic articles.
