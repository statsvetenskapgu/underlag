SF2321 H24 – Syllabus/Kursguide

Welcome to SF2321 Applied Statistical Analysis!

This course is given as either a singel subject course at the second cycle or as an elective course at the Master's Programmes in Criminology, European Studies, International Administation and Global Governance, Political Communication, Political Science and Sociology, all within the Faculty of Social Sciences.

You can see the reading list and the schedule further down on this page. The updated course guide will be found here two weeks before the course starts.
