Course registration

For those of you who are Erasmus-students you won´t be able to register yourself. You will be registered by the course administrator after you have confirmed that you are joining the course. A confirmation request will be sent to you by email a few days before the course begins.

For the swedish students you register yourself on Ladok. The registration is only open between 29 november - 2 december.
If you have any problems to register for the course or if you have missed the registration deadline, please contact the course administrator.

Examinations

This course contains both a group oral presentation, three seminars with seminar paper submissions and an individual research essay. You will find more information about the different part of the course in the course guide when you are registered.

 

Plagiarism

Plagiarism means that you use someone elses paper or work and present it as your own, without using correct references to the author or where you have collected the information. Plagiarism is under no circumstances allowed and can lead to suspension. It´s important that you take part of the information about academic writing and plagerism, which you can find both in the course guide and through the University Library´s digital resources.
