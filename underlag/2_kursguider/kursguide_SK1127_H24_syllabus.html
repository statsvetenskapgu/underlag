Course start

The course starts with a course introduction on November 5 at 10.15 in room 420, Annedalsseminariet, Seminariegatan 1A (Campus Linné).

 

Course registration

You can only register for the course between the following dates: October 29-30. You register yourself via Ladok and it is not until you have registered that you are a student on the course. If you fail to register during those dates, contact course administrator Renate Valaas Sørvik as soon as possible (svep@pol.gu.se) 

As an Erasmus student you will be registered by the course administrator after you have confirmed that you are joining the course. You will get an confirmation request sent to you through email a few days before the course starts.

Course evaluation

All students on the course will receive an email from GU enkät-survey, survey.no-replay@gu.se, with an URL to the course evaluation. We are very grateful if you fill it out. The survey is strictly anonymous.

 

Plagiarism

Plagiarism means that you use someone elses paper or work and present it as your own, without using correct references to the author or where you have collected the information. Plagiarism is under no circumstances allowed and can lead to suspension. It's important that you take part of the information about academic writing and plagerism, which you can find both in the course guide and through the University Library´s digital resources.
