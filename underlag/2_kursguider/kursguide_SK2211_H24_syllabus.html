SK2211 H24 The Performance of Democracies

Welcome to the course The Performance of Democracies! You can find the literature and the schedule below. The updated course guide will be published here two weeks before the course starts.

The introduction

The introduction to the course will take place the 2nd of September in D250 Sprängkullsgatan, 10:15-12:00. There you will meet the main instructors Amy Alexander and Georgios Xezorakis for further introduction and course logistics.

Registration

To be able to see all the pages on this learning platform, Canvas, you must be registered for the course. You register yourself online on Ladok one week before the introduction (26th to 27th of August). More information about registration can be found here.

If you don't have a Swedish personal number, please contact the course administrator wilma.holmestrand@gu.se and she will help you.

Contact us
