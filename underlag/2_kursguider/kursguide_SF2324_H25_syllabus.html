Registration

To be able to see all the pages on Canvas you must be registered for the course.You register yourself in Ladok one week before the course starts. You can read more about the registration here.

If you don't have a Swedish personal number you need to visit the Servicecenter and activate your student account first, after that you can register for the course.

Please contact the course administrator (master@pol.gu.se), if you are having trouble with registration.

Course evaluation

We ask you to please fill in the course evaluation at the end of your course. You'll find it in the course Cavas page menu, under "Course Evaluations". The evaluations help us develop and improve our courses and your contribution is valuable! 

You can read reports on previous cohorts of this course and others, in the publicly available Canvas course "Kursrapporter/Course Reports". Note that you may have to look under both of the modules "Master's Programme in Political Science..." and "Master's Programme in International Administration and Global Governance" to find your course, as some courses are included in several programmes. 

 

Find your way on Canvas
