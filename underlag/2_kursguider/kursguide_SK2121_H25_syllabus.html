Welcome to the course Political Science Analysis! This is the first course at the Master's Programme in Political Science & Master's Programme in Political Science: Environmental Governance and Behavior. The course is also open for exchange students. 

Introduction

The introduction to the programme will take place on September 1 in the University Main Building, Vasaparken, Vasaplatsen.

Only for Maps students at 10:00-11:00.

Only for MapsE students at 11:15-12:15.

Later the same day (September 1), you will meet the main instructor Frida Boräng in room A012 Sprängkullsgatan 19, 13:15-15:00 for the course introduction.

Registration

To be able to see all the pages on Canvas you must be registered for the course.You register yourself in Ladok one week before the course starts. You can read more about the registration here.

If you don't have a Swedish personal number you need to visit the Servicecenter and activate your student account first, after that you can register for the course.

Please contact the course administrator, Sandra Grahn (master@pol.gu.se), if you have trouble with the registration.

Course evaluation

We ask you to please fill in the course evaluation at the end of your course. You'll find it in the course Cavas page menu, under "Course Evaluations". The evaluations help us develop and improve our courses and your contribution is valuable! 

You can read reports on previous cohorts of this course and others, in the publicly available Canvas course "Kursrapporter/Course Reports". Note that you may have to look under both of the modules "Master's Programme in Political Science..." and "Master's Programme in International Administration and Global Governance" to find your course, as some courses are included in several programmes. 

 

Find your way in Canvas

  • Canvas is based on Modules, see to the left.
  • When you are registered on the course you can log in to Canvas and find more information about the course. The teachers will upload assigments, PowerPoint presentations and other useful documents or material.
  • Canvas is also the place where you also submit the assignments. There is a folder for each submission.
