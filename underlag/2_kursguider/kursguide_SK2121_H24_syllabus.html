SK2121 H24 Political Science Analysis

Welcome to the course Political Science Analysis. This is the first course at the Master's Programme in Political Science and Master's Programme in Political Science: Environmental Governance and Behavior. The course is also given as a single subject course.

 

Introduction

The introduction of the programme will take place the 2nd of September 2024 in the University main building, Vasaparken, Vasaplatsen.

Only for Maps students at 10:00-11:00.

Only for MapsE students at 11:15-12:15.

Later the same day, the 2nd of September you will meet the main instructor Frida Boräng in B228 Sprängkullsgatan, 13:15-15:00 for the course introduction.

 

Registration

To be able to see all the pages on this learning platform, Canvas, you must be registered for the course.You register yourself online on Ladok one week before the introduction. More information about registration can be found here.

If you don't have a Swedish personal number you will need to visit the Servicecenter in person and activate your student account first, and then you can register yourself online.

Contact the course and programme administrator, Wilma Holmestrand, wilma.holmestrand@gu.se, if you have difficulties with your registration.

 

Contact us
