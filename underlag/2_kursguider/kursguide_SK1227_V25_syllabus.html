Introduction

Course Registration

To access all the pages on Canvas, you must be registered for the course.

  • You register online in Ladok between 13 and 14 January. More information about registration can be found here.
  • If you do not have a Swedish personal identity number, you need to arrive in Sweden and activate your student account in person at a Servicecenter before you can register.

If you have difficulties with your registration, please contact the education administrator, Sara Tacchetti, at svep@pol.gu.se.

Plagiarism

Plagiarism means using someone else's work and presenting it as your own without proper references to the author or the source. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and can lead to suspension. It is important that you take part of the information about academic writing and plagiarism, which you can find both in the course guide and through the University Library's digital resources.
