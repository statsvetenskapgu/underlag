AG2110 H24 Theoretical and Historical Perspectives on Global Governance

Welcome to the course Theoretical and Historical Perspectives on Global Governance. This the first course on the Master's Programme in International Administration and Global Governance.

Introduction

The introduction of the programme will take place the 2nd of September 2024 in the University main building, Vasaparken, Vasaplatsen at 13:15-16:00.

The 3rd of September you will meet the main instructor Amy Alexander in B225 Sprängkullsgatan, 13:15-15:00 for the introduction to the course and course logistics.

Registration

To be able to see all the pages on this learning platform, Canvas, you must be registered for the course. You register yourself online on Ladok one week before the introduction. More information about registration can be found here. If you don't have a Swedish personal number, you will be registered on the day of the introduction by the course administrator.

Contact us

Please note If you want to contact the teacher or the admin staff, please send an e-mail, do not use any other message services.

Main instructor: Amy Alexander, e-mail: amy.alexander@gu.se
