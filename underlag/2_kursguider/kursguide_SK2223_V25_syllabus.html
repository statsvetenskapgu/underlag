SK2223 V25 Environmental Problems within Social Science: From Tragedy of the Commons to Planetary Boundaries

The course is taken by students at the Department of Political Science and free standing students, it is a 15 credits course.

Course introduction

The course starts on 20th of January, 2025:

Intoduction at 9:00-10:00 in D138, Göran Duus-Otterström.

First lecture at 10:15-12:00 in D138, Sverker Carlsson Jagers.

Registration

To be able to see all the pages on this learning platform, Canvas, you must be registered for the course.You register yourself online on Ladok one week before the introduction. More information about registration can be found here.

If you don't have a Swedish personal number you will need to visit the Servicecenter in person and activate your student account first, and then you can register yourself online.

Please contact the course administrator Wilma Holmestrand (wilma.holmestrand@gu.se), if you have difficulties with your registration.

Course information

When you are registered and can log in to Canvas, you will get access to all parts of the course. The teachers will upload exercises, PowerPoint presentations and other useful documents or material in the modules.
